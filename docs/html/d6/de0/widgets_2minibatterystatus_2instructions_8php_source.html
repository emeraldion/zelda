3 <h3>Install</h3>
4 
5 <p>
6 
7 Drop MiniBatteryStatus in /Library/Widgets/ in your home folder. Show Dashboard, click the Plus sign to display the Widget Bar and click the widget&rsquo;s icon in the Widget Bar to open it.
8 
9 </p>
10 
11 <h3>Usage</h3>
12 <p>
13 MiniBatteryStatus is already configured to start monitoring your laptop&rsquo;s primary battery. When the laptop is plugged, the widget will
14 assume a deep blue color; moreover a small status icon tells you wheter the battery is just bypassed or it is charging. When you&rsquo;re running
15 unplugged, the widget switches to color mode and changes from deep green (100% of charge) to deep red (0% of charge). Of course, in between
16 you&rsquo;ll see literally thousands of different gorgeous gradations.
17 </p>
18 <p class="centered">
19  <a href="/assets/images/software/widgets/minibatterystatus/arcoiris-realsize.png">
20  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/minibatterystatus/arcoiris.png" alt="Virtually thousands of different colors" /></a>
21 </p>
22 <p>
23 MiniBatteryStatus features four different visualizations. The default visualization is the remaining charge percentage.
24 </p>
25 <p class="centered">
26  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/minibatterystatus/25.png" alt="Charge percentage visualization" />
27 </p>
28 <p>
29 Click on the front of the widget to change the visualization to the remaining time. You may need to wait some time to allow the widget to calculate the time.
30 </p>
31 <p class="centered">
32  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/minibatterystatus/26.png" alt="Remaining time visualization" />
33 </p>
34 <p>
35 Click again to change the visualization to battery details. Here you can read the current and maximum capacity and the completed cycles.
36 </p>
37 <p class="centered">
38  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/minibatterystatus/13.png" alt="Details visualization" />
39 </p>
40 <p>
41 Click one last time to set the plain visualization. You will just see the translucent charge bar. Click again to start the cycle.
42 </p>
43 <p class="centered">
44  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/minibatterystatus/19.png" alt="Empty visualization" />
45 </p>
