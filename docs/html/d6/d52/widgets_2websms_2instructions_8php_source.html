68 <h3 id="websms">WebSMS</h3>
69 <p class="centered">
70  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/websms/websms_front-1.4.png" alt="WebSMS interface" />
71 </p>
72 <p>
73  WebSMS widget represents a small cellular phone, with a big screen
74  and three buttons. The central one sends the message and performs some other
75  actions, the left button launches the web browser to this guide and the right
76  one flips the widget to show the back configuration pane.
77 </p>

79 <h3>Configuration</h3>
80 <h4 id="call_settings">Call settings</h4>
81 <p class="centered">
82  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/websms/websms_preferences-call.png" alt="WebSMS call preferences" />
83 </p>
84 <p>
85  Before using WebSMS, you&rsquo;ll have to enter the username and password to
86  access your provider&rsquo;s service.
87  You can use the <b>sign</b> field to append a signature to every message
88  you send (actual support depends on plugin implementations).
89 </p>
90 <p>
91  You can insert the recipient&rsquo;s phone number either directly, or choosing
92  it from a dropdown menu. Contacts are retrieved from those featuring a mobile
93  phone number in the Address Book.
94 </p>
95 <h4 id="network_settings">Network settings</h4>
96 <p class="centered">
97  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/websms/websms_preferences-network.png" alt="WebSMS network preferences" />
98 </p>
99 <p>
100  If you are behind a proxy, you will have to inform WebSMS of that. Here you
101  can fill the proxy type, host name and port, authentication keys and scheme.
102  If you don&rsquo;t know what a proxy is, you&rsquo;d have to leave those fields
103  blank
104 </p>

108 </p>
109 <p>

113 <h4 id="international_settings">International settings</h4>
114 <p class="centered">
115  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/websms/websms_preferences-international.png" alt="WebSMS international preferences" />
116 </p>
117 <p>
118  Choose your country. The country code will be used to filter services not available
119  in your country and to provide a country code to those services that require one.
120 </p>
121 <h4 id="su_settings">Software Update settings</h4>
122 <p class="centered">
123  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/websms/websms_preferences-software-update.png" alt="WebSMS software update preferences" />
124 </p>
125 <p>
126  Tracking software updates is always a pain in the a** (not to mention development!).
127  This panel lets you find available updates for the installed plugins. Click <strong>Check</strong>
128  to retrieve a list of the newest plugins, choose the ones
129  you&rsquo;d like to update, then click <strong>Update</strong>.
130  They will be downloaded, replaced and automatically loaded for immediate use.
131 </p>
132 <h3 id="composing">Composing</h3>
133 <p class="centered">
134  <img src="/assets/images/software/widgets/websms/websms_compose-1.4.png" alt="Message composing" />
135 </p>
136 <p>
137  Write your message. You&rsquo;ll see how many available characters are left in
