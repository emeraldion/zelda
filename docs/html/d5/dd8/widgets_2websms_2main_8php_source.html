15 <h3>Features</h3>
16 <p id="feature-addressbook">
17  WebSMS is integrated with your Address Book: all your mobile contacts
18  are available in the contacts list. But there&rsquo;s more: WebSMS will automatically complete
19  the number that you are typing if it matches one of your contacts.
20 </p>
21 <p id="feature-secure">
22  WebSMS securely stores your account passwords in the system Keychain, so they are safely
23  hidden from malicious access.
24 </p>

28 </p>
