11 <p>
12  Singular helps you to find and remove duplicate files on your Mac. Drop files
13  &amp; folders on its window or its Dock icon, choose files with the open panel,
14  it makes no difference: it finds duplicate files, shows you the results and
15  lets you move them to the Trash just in a click of the mouse.
16 </p>

18 <h3>Features</h3>
19 <p id="feature-clean">
20  Singular can help you to find duplicate files on a given set of files and folders using
21  four different criteria: MD5 hash, SHA-1 hash, file size and file name.
22 </p>
23 <p id="feature-advanced">
24  For the power user, Singular has a bunch of advanced settings to customize the way it
25  scans for duplicates.
26 </p>
27 <p id="feature-rescue">
28  Singular by default keeps a copy for each duplicate file. That way you can safely delete
29  all the other copies.
30  And what if you change your mind? Singular keeps a log of what it had moved to the Trash
31  and its original location.
32 </p>
33 <p id="feature-quickscans">
34  Do you always scan the same folders in search of duplicates? You will love Quick Scans.
35  Group folders in a Quick Scan and launch it with a single keystroke!
36 </p>
37 <p id="feature-swupdate">
38  Singular uses <a href="http://sparkle.andymatuschak.org" class="external">Sparkle</a>
39  to handle automatic software updates.
40  Never worry to check for updates anymore!
41 </p>
42 <p id="feature-widget">
43  Singular comes with a nifty widget that lets you drop files and folders from the Dashboard.
44 </p>
