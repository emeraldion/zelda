3 <p>
4  Drop Moments into ~/Library/Widgets (that's the Library folder located in your home folder, preferred) or /Library/Widgets.
5  Activate Dashboard, drag the widget to your screen and enjoy!
6 </p>
7 
8 <p>
9  To load a photo in the widget, simply drag a file on it from the Finder or iPhoto, or a web image from Safari or your favorite web browser. Moments will load and display it. To change frame styles, double click the widget. To remove the photo, just hit Backspace.
10 </p>
11 
12 <p>
13  You can load as many pictures as you like in every instance of the widget. To start a slideshow, hit the space bar, hit it another time to pause. You can move forward and backwards between the pictures using the arrow keys; to delete a picture you no longer want, hit backspace.
14 </p>

21 <p>
22  Turn the widget to show its preferences. <b>Size</b> can be chosen between five discrete values: micro, tiny, medium, large and huge; the widget will resize with a nice animation.
23  If you'd like to remove the glass reflection effect, you can deselect the <b>With Glass</b> checkbox. Slideshow controls only apply when the widget holds more than a photo.
24  <b>Slideshow interval</b> is the time in seconds after which a photo is changed. <b>Auto Slideshow</b> allows the slideshow to automatically begin once you turn the widget to its front.
25 </p>
