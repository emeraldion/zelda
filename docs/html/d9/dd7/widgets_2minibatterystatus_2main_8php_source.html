12  MiniBatteryStatus widget monitors battery status and charge percentage. It displays colorful information
13  to quickly notice your battery conditions.
14  </p>
15  <p>
16  The widget assumes different colors depending on the remaining charge of the battery. It is also aware of
17  battery charging and plugged status. User can choose if the widget should display percentage, remaining
18  time, capacity (current/maximum) and cycle count or just the charge gauge.
19  </p>
20  <p>
21  MiniBatteryStatus is definitely a must have for all Mac laptop users.
22  </p>
